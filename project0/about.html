About Me

Passion took me at this point of my life.

Javier Sánchez

In 2015 I presented an idea at a Startup Weekend contest. I went there with a business role and made my pitch.

At the beginning no one appeared very interested in my idea. There were others ideas more fascinating, like AR related, some mobile apps, etc.

The idea I promoted was a saas for business marketing, although at this time I only had some html, css, and wordpress implementation skills gained from blogging (but almost no real coding skills).

Finally I got a team and with every contribution made by team mates and mentors, the idea evolved to a more appealing one.

On Sunday we were the winners of the contest. I got hooked.

After years of corporate jobs, I felt like I have found my way. A passion burning inside my heart was born.

But...the dream was made of thin air. And after two months the team was disolved.

After a time, I felt I needed to learn programming.

I felt that I wanted to be able to build all this crazy ideas that were coming in regulary to my mind.

And here I am. A CFO with lots of experience in finance and leading financial and administration teams, learning to code and trying to thrive in tech.

Why?

It is passion wich took me here. I am just following the drum of my heart.
